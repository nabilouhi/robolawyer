Applicant

        

Individual

This section refers to applicants who are individual persons only. If the applicant is an organisation, please select Organisation above.



If your application is declared admissible by the Court, your name will appear on the documents related to your case that are published by the court. These documents are publicly available. You can request not to disclose your identity to the public. The request has to be sent as an attachment to the Court and has to present reasons why making your identity public will affect/cause harm to the applicant (or to others?). The court decides whether you are granted anonymity or not. You should state in the request whether, in the event of anonymity being authorised by the President of the Chamber, you wish to be designated by your initials or by a single letter (e.g. “X”, “Y” or “Z”).
